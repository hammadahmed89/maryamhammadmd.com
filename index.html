About Dr. Hammad

Compassionate Care
for Every Patient

Dr. Maryam Hammad is an internal medicine resident physician practicing at Sylvan Care Center in Modesto, California. She brings a thorough, patient-centered approach to diagnosing and managing a wide range of adult medical conditions, with a strong focus on preventive health, chronic disease management, and long-term wellness. Dr. Hammad is committed to building meaningful relationships with her patients and providing individualized care for the Modesto community and surrounding areas.
